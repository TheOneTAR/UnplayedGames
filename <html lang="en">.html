Unplayed Games

This is a handy (not really) little tool that tells you how long and how many people it would take, on average, to play all the unplayed games in your BGG collection.

It assumes that your collection is somewhat up to date, and that you log your plays. It also assumes you're very social and only ever play one game with someone. Or would that make you unsocial?
